# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
## Do’s
1.Use privacy settings to protect personal information.

2..Think before posting any content.

3.Be respectful and polite in online communication.

4.Share useful and positive content.

5.Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

## Don’ts
1.Do not share personal information like address or phone number.

2.Do not post offensive or inappropriate content.

3.Do not spread fake news or misinformation.

4.Do not use weak passwords.

5.Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.

## Poor Digital Communication (100–150 words)
Poor digital communication can cause misunderstandings and confusion.
Sending emails without a subject line or greeting looks unprofessional.
Using rude or unclear language can create a negative impression.
Posting inappropriate content on social media can damage a person’s reputation.
Sharing wrong information online can mislead people.
Not checking grammar and spelling makes communication look careless.
Digital communication should always be clear, polite, and professional.
Reviewing messages before sending can prevent communication problems.
